About me

Click on the bar to see the text

My name is Edwin Ochoa and as a teen growing up in the L.A. area, I've experienced many things that have molded my character into what it is today. One thing I really like doing is writing. It doesn't matter what it is I write down, as long as I get my thoughts onto paper. I first discovered I liked writing when I was in elementary school; we were given assignments where we could write about anything and I just let my imagination run wild. I started being exposed to the worst side of L.A. by the time I reached middle school; I started doing things I wasn't supposed to do, such as stealing, getting into fights and other mischievous things. These things messed up my mental state but I found writing down what was on my mind to be an outlet for my emotions. Since this discovery, I've stopped getting into troyble and I've been writing as often as I can
