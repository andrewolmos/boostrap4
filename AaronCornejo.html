Hi, my name is Aaron Cornejo. I'm 16 years old born in Torrance and raised in Carson. In my family I live with 3 brothers and 1 sister, and with out them I would not be the person I am today. Growing up I was always a daring and adventurous person as I always liked to go out and try to learn new things. That's where my hobbies formed. Some of my hobbies consist of Skateboarding, Playing Video Games and Listening to Music. I can happily say that I get to enjoy these things everyday of my life. Going to school in my early teen ages, I was always shy and didn't like to communicate with others. However in the last few years of my life I have grown as a more Smarter, Athletic and Happy person. That leaves me to how I am today as I always notice myself constantly Smiling, Laughing and Having Fun with the new people I meet every day.
